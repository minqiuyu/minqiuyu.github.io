Latitude and Temperature

As one would expect, temperatures are consistently high at 0 degrees latitude, which is Earth's equator, and lower for positive latitudes as the Earth wobbles on its axis of rotation. Note that negative latitudes (The southern hemisphere) also have high temperatures than would be expected for the month of September; this can be attributed to the southern hemisphere beginning to change its season to summer.
