Latitude and Wind Speed (in MPH)

It appears that the presence of many active storm systems in the northern hemisphere has also influenced wind speeds, particularly the hurricanes that affected North America in the fall of 2018. While the southern hemisphere is outright placid, the northern hemisphere has a very high number of fast winds recorded, very likely from the aforementioned storms.
